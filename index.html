Senior national selection

The 67th Senior National Kabaddi Championship will commence on 2nd March 2020 and culminate on 6th March 2020, and will take place at the Poornima University in Jaipur, Rajasthan. The tournament will play host to 31 teams from the men's category and 30 teams from the women's category. Big names like Pardeep Narwal, Pawan Sehrawat, Ajay Thakur and Rahul Chaudhari among others will be representing their respective states/employers.
